# PNET Community Contribution Protocol Legal Disclaimer

Last updated: 2026-06-27
Status: Draft for legal review. Not legal advice. TestNet MVP only.

The PNET Community Contribution Protocol is designed as a non-custodial system for recording verified participation in approved external activities, including Kryptex-style compute participation and Honeygain / JumpTask-style bandwidth-sharing evidence.

Accepted participation may result in app-local access and reputation credits. These credits are records inside the contribution application. They are not PNET tokens, not deposits, not yield, not passive income, not guaranteed value, and not investment returns.

## Important Notices

- Participation is voluntary.
- Credits represent access and reputation only.
- Credits do not represent equity, ownership, profit-sharing rights, dividends, treasury claims, or any form of investment in ProfitNet, PNET, or any associated entity.
- No user is guaranteed financial return, token value appreciation, income, liquidity, listing support, or market outcome as a result of participation.
- External evidence, such as redacted screenshots or activity files, should be hashed before public recording.
- Only hashes, metadata, and review decisions should be recorded on-chain.
- The protocol does not custody user funds, tokens, private keys, recovery phrases, or signer material.
- Mining and bandwidth-sharing activities can involve real-world costs, including electricity, hardware wear, internet usage, and service-specific limitations. Users are solely responsible for evaluating those costs.

## No Investment, Financial, Legal, Or Tax Advice

Nothing in the protocol, dossier, website, frontend, documentation, or related communications constitutes investment, financial, legal, accounting, tax, or professional advice.

Users should conduct their own due diligence and consult qualified professionals before participating.

## Jurisdictional Notice

Laws and rules regarding digital assets, contribution systems, rewards, data handling, and public communications vary by jurisdiction. Users are responsible for understanding and following applicable laws in their location.

## Privacy Notice

Raw screenshots, private dashboards, payout values, private wallet screenshots, account emails, device identifiers, IP addresses, cookies, session data, and private account details should not be published in this repository or recorded directly on-chain.

The intended public record is limited to:

- activity category,
- reviewer decision,
- receipt hash,
- evidence hash,
- account-reference hash,
- transaction ID,
- non-sensitive metadata.
