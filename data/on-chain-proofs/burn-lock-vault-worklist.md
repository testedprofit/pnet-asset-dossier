## Proof Packet Checklist

Every proof packet must include:

- claim,
- source URL,
- capture date,
- capture round where available,
- transaction ID or account/app address,
- raw amount,
- display amount,
- method,
- reviewer,
- status,
- limitations.

## Current Gate Status

Burn, lock, vault, LP, and circulating supply claims remain blocked until proof packets exist.
